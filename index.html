Collaboration At Its Core

The Members Core Alliance provides credit unions with a solid, reliable and user friendly core processing platform through a cooperative effort among those you already know and trust...your peers! Created through the collaboration of 8 Georgia credit unions, MCA enables participating credit unions to outsource their core data processor to a common platform while streamlining operations, lowering expenses, and easing their overall compliance burden. Using a credit union service organization (CUSO) structure to centralize management, MCA has partnered with Fiserv to create an all-encompassing data processing solution.
